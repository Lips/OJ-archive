Jeffrey Lewis (solo acoustic)
Orlando, Florida - Sat May 28, 2011.
House show at 13865 Belvin Court, Orlando, FL, 32826.
All-ages, $5 suggested donation.
Doors at 8, Victor Florence at 9, Jeffrey Lewis (solo acoustic) at 10, The Jabronis at 11.

INFO: "Belvin Court is a cul-de-sac and this house is the one with the basketball hoop in the driveway and the two large evergreens at the end of the drive. Try as hard as you can to carpool because there's a limited space on the road to park. Once the driveway is full you can park around the edge of the cul-de-sac and along the road, just don't block any driveways. Call 321-890-7786 if you need more info."
