Mary Bluestocking Opening: RBPMW Member Show

Fri, 10/14/2011 - 6:00pm
Location: 

Blackburn 20|20 Gallery EFA bldg. 323 West 39th St, 5th fl,
New York, NY 10018

Important update! RBPMW gallery hours are more restricted than originally posted! See the gallery hours below for the corrected times.

You are all invited to the the Robert Blackburn Printmaking Workshop's Annual Member Show where I'm hanging one brand new piece of mine. And likely a second one too (either also new or an old one that's rarely seen in person).

This is a rare opportunity to see my work as I've never shown in NYC before. There'll be pieces by numerous artists from a wide variety of backgrounds and diversity of vision.

Opening Reception: Friday, 10/14/11 6pm-9pm
Closing Reception: Friday, 11/4/11 6pm-8pm

IMPORTANT NOTE!: If you see the show at a time other than the reception call ahead to make sure the RBPMW staff knows you're coming so they'll be ready to grant you access to the gallery!: 646.416.6226
The shop itself where the staff usually is is on the 2nd fl. (they let you into the gallery which is on the 5th.)

RBPMW gallery hours are:
Closed Mondays
Tuesday-Friday: 10 am - 5:45 pm
Saturday - Sunday: 10 am - 5:45 pm

I will post other participating artist's names in the "News" section of my website when I get a names list in October.

For more info:
RBPMW's site: http://www.efanyc.org/rbpmw/
My site: www.marybluestocking.com

If any of you do see it when I'm not around, drop me a line and tell me you did. I'd love to know.
