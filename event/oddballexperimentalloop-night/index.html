A night of, apparently, weird shit:

7:30 La Loop
8:15 - Johnny Dydo
9:30 - Barnyard Boys
1015 The Happy Rappies
11-Jon Glovin
11:45-Yossarian Feedback

In the words of Ben:

This of you coming to the Sidewalk this Wednesday are probably planning to check out the Happy Rappies, Johnny Dydo or Jon Glovin (maybe me?). I wanted to give a quick shout out to the two acts you may not know:

The Barnyard Boys are a fantastic trio that played Sidewalk a few months ago and blew everyone away. They have a zillion great songs: Guided By Voices if they grew up on Long Island eating too many cheese burgers and dressing like Sha Na Na. Highly recommended.

http://www.myspace.com/thebarnyardboys I like "Don't Disrespect the Wizard"

Also, kicking off the night is La Loop, the stage name for Annie Levy who plays in Peter Dizozza's band. She's fantastic and shouldn't be missed. [no website link available]

Also, Alex P--who is usually behind the board and plays his own brand of roots rock songs--is DJing in between sets as Gil Damage. He's a studio geek and has concocted some really cool avant-garde tape loop stuff.

It's going to be a good one...hope to c some of you there!

Wednesday, November 3: YOSSARIAN FEEDBACK PRESENTS "A Night of Weird and Kooky Stuff" 7:30-La Loop (feat. Annie Levy), 8:15 - Johnny Dydo, 9:30-The Barnyard Boys, 10:15-The Happy Rappies, 11-Jon Glovin, 11:45-Yossarian Feedback. All night in between sets: Tape-loop DJ experiments by Gill Damage
