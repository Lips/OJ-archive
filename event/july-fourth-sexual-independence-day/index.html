detailed workshop info::::

=============================================

“7 steps to better boundaries” presented by Betsy Cohen
This workshop will focus on boundaries related to romance/sex,
with a special Q&A after. Participants will be invited to ask questions about their boundaries and Betsy will offer intuitive readings and feedback.

=============================================

“Body Painting” (ongoing) led by Michael Gates
In-party activity (neither performance nor workshop).
This activity will aim to provide a fast, fun and temporary way for
party-goers to re-imagine their appearance, using a limited palette for thematic unity and within expressive diversity.

Nudity is very optional. Face, arm and/or leg painting is fine.
The more clothes removed, the bigger the canvas for painting;
the more paint applied, the less naked one will feel.

Interactivity will be very high. As activity-leader, I will offer to paint others, and I will sport samples of work, but party-goers will be welcome and encouraged to self-apply or paint volunteers.

Michael Gates is an advocate of sexual independence and body liberation. He is bisexually polyamorous and has mad respect for the Bonobo Chimpanzees. Naked yoga keeps his bread buttered. Discovering the difference between orgasm and ejaculation made an enormous difference in his life; ask him how.

=============================================

www.leatherconrad.com

=============================================

"Hair Brushing: Sensual or Sexual?" provided by Toby Vann
Toby Vann will offer hair brushing while engaging in dialogue about the difference between sensuality and sexuality. Toby would like to video tape participants who are comfortable and willing. B.Y.O.H.B. if you don’t want cooties.
