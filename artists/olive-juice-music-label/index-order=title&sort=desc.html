Olive Juice Music (label)

Submitted by MMM on Mon, 09/28/2009 - 4:59pm
  • Olive Juice Music (label) Website
  • Olive Juice Music (label) on MySpace

Olive Juice Music is a D.I.Y. label, studio, and mail-order distributor, based in New York City, interested in helping people who are in the developmental stages of trying to do something with their art connect with people who are in the developmental stages of appreciating it. OJM is not a traditional record label. We realized after a couple of years into this that funding the occasional release is fun but a better way to reach a wider variety of artists is to operate more as a distributor and promoter of music. With that being said we occasionally delve into bank rolling the odd compilation or "friend" cover project.
