Schwervon! and Toby Goodshank - Fall Tour '06 EP - CDR

Submitted by MMM on Thu, 01/07/2010 - 7:07pm
  • Schwervon! and Toby Goodshank - Fall Tour '06 EP - CDR by Schwervon!
  • Catalog #: OJ-0058

We are happy to reissue a limited edition of this fun little tour EP of two of OJ's hottest artists covering two of each other's songs. Includes a collaborative rendition of Clampdown by the Clash including a rare guest appearance by Sharky from Pantsuit. (2006)

01. Death of My Enemies
02. Flaming Dragonfly
03. Change Your Ways
04. Sore Eyes
05. Clampdown

Other artists on this release

Toby Goodshank
Nan Turner
Double Deuce
Major Matt Mason USA
Pantsuit
