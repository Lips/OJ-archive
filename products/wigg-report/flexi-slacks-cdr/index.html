Proof that Durham really does love us the Wigg Report's sophomore long player preserves their classic beach blanket, road house stompin' DIY guitar, sax, drum indie pop trio stylings with occasional help from the odd trumpet or moog synth this time. Plus they cover an Agent Orange song. God I love this band! (2006)
