Temper Tantrums Cause Delays - CD by Thomas Patrick Maguire
Catalog #: OJD-0295
List Price: $10.00
"Thomas Patrick Maguire puts everything great about 90s rock, minus the bullshit, in a modern light. 'Temper Tantrums Cause Delays' is the best album I've heard in 2010, pants down." - Brook Pridemore.
TPM's fourth full length album is his most accessible work to date. Backed up by his record producers, Jon Sebastian (The Color Wheels) and Brian Speaker (The Everybody Knows), plus Elastic No-No Band's Doug Johnson on drums, Thomas leaves behind home recording without losing the intimacy of his earlier albums. Once you've heard this album, if you don't have most of the songs stuck in your head afterward, then you weren't really listening. (2010)

Costello - CDR by Dan Costello
Catalog #: OJD-0296
List Price: $10.00
Written on a month-long solo train sabbatical across the US and Canada. The first album recorded at Brooklyn Tea Party, in the hot summer of 2008. The band is Dan Costello, Rachel Devlin, Michael David Campbell, Brook Pridemore and Eric Wolfson. Guest appearances by Vincent Cacchione (of Soft Black), Casey Holford and Ben Godwin (who produced it). Mastered by Major Matt Mason USA at Olive Juice Studios. Includes "Poison", "Tape Recorder", and the Stephin Merritt-esque "Breakup Song for Brook Pridemore to Sing". (2008)

Recession Songs - CDR by Dan Costello
Catalog #: OJD-0297
List Price: $10.00
A folk album in the tradition of Woody Guthrie's Dust Bowl Ballads. Recorded live in simple lo-fi arrangements, this album was released as a free download and recently became available on CD. Includes "The Boy With the Gun" and "We Are All Around". Appearances by Rachel Devlin and Debe Dalton. Recorded and Produced by Brian Speaker at Brooklyn Tea Party. There is not one curse word on it! (2009)

Lost and Found - CDR by The Fools
Catalog #: OJD-0301
List Price: $10.00
The Fools are a Brooklyn based musical duo. They have been described as "sit back and float folk" (Adam Thomas--plug in music) and "a foot massage for your ears" (CBX). They released their first studio album, Lost and Found, in late 2009. In the summer of 2010 the Fools took their music on the road.

Capitol Punishment - CD by Kung Fu Crimewave
Catalog #: OJ-0073
List Price: $10.00
Kung Fu Crimewave's long anticipated debut album capitol punishment is finally here, marking the dawn of a new era in rock and roll. The band's unique brand of post apocalyptic maximalism is showcased perfectly, and includes old fan favorites such as Sentigar and Kill For the Side, as well as the blistering pop anthems Merry-Go-Round and Monster Ballad. Produced by Dibson T. Hoffwieler and mixed and mastered by Major Matt Mason, the album achieves a polished sound without losing any of the primal energy that makes Kung Fu Crimewave so entertaining. (2010)
