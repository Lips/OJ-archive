After a month away, I've posted 2 new podcasts, both of which stray from the normal interview format. They are 2 halves of a field recording of my performance at the BLACK NO-NO'S CD release house show back on April 28, 2012. Doug Johnson was nice enough to stick his recording device out there and capture the music.

Monday, I posted the larger chunk, a 25-minute mega-medley I performed solo, of Elastic No-No Band songs and covers. All the songs I did can be found on our albums somewhere. http://elasticnonoband.com

Episode 6 hi-res: http://weemaykmusic.bandcamp...

Episode 6 compressed mp3: http://wereyouthinking.podbean...

Today, I posted the second half of the show, where I had Debe Dalton come up and we did 4 folk songs together. Jon Berger also has a few funny asides from the audience.

Episode 6.5 hi-res: http://weemaykmusic.bandcamp...

Episode 6.5 compressed mp3: http://wereyouthinking.podbean...
