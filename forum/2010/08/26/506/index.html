WYSIWYG acts as a host/integrator, and then you drop in the editors in as plugins, it seems to be the way this particular clusterfark is wrapping up, as far as I can see. You still have a choice of 10 editors. :)

CKeditor has simple config files that allow you to define your toolbars.
config.toolbar_OjBasic = [
['Bold','Italic'],
[''Blockquote','NumberedList','BulletedList']
['Link','Unlink']

Each [...] section is a toolbar chunk for visual grouping.
Though I'm not sure if yr supposed to make your own toolbars, or edit the defaults like DrupalBasic, etc.. that come preloaded in the config file.
