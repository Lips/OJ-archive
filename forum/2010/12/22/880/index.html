Science is not a set of

Science is not a set of answers. It is a method for trying to figure things out. This is different than religion, which has set rules and answers.

But, I do have an issue with putting too much faith into the hands of scientists.

C.S. Lewis touched on this in The Hideous Strength, the idea that science could be embraced as a faith or truth and be used as a tool for fascism. I love that book.

Science is rewriting their own rules everytime a new discovery is made.

Practicing true scientific method, one needs to be prepared to discover that one's entire hypothesis/theory can be disproven. There is no truth in science, there is only theories that are mostly likely based on evidence. It can be tough for any scientist who has devoted his/her life to a certain theory to find, one day, that they are most likely wrong. But that's the breaks.
