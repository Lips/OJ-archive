Anyone around here remember Mike Rocklin? Old guard Antifolker. I liked him. Good performer. Didn't live in the city. Played harmonica and guitar. He was a part of the 'Hoots in the mid 90's, and probably much earlier, too.

I'm pretty sure he was posting on the old OJ board for a while there, unless it was the DamnFine board before that. He could be a little confrontational or arch, but he had good intentions underneath it all. I also remember that his health was in a scary state, at least for a while. (I forget the specifics.)

Anyone hear from him? I hope he's OK.

He was recording onto wax cylinders for a time, and I'd love to catch up with him on that stuff.
