Mac system software isn't

Mac system software isn't protected, you can install it from borrowed discs or whatever. There is a slight quirk though.. I *think* there are two colors of install discs. Grey & white. They grey are for that model specifically, and the white are "universal." It might be the other way around, or maybe they've changed it. Anyway, if the discs are for another model, it won't cause harm, it just won't install.

I don't think you need snow leopard either, leopard should work fine.

Alternately, get an older touch, they're all quite nice. The camera is probably the biggest difference.. some apps won't run, but you'll probably never notice the diff. Ben got a 1st gen on my recommendation, I think he's happy with it.
