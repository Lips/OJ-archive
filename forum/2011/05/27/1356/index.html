Projector

Looking for projectors is a *total* pain in the arse.
A million models, a million weird acronyms, etc.

I looked through an article that listed the best travel projectors from 2008/9, and then set up a saved search on ebay (If you don't know how to do this, it's simply awesome). I picked up a Viewsonic PJ538 (2000 Lumens, LCD, 4 lbs) with about 25 hours on it for $200 and change.
It's a wide throw, so it needs to be close to the projected surface, but it's great for me.

I strongly suggest finding the model you want (argg), and then setting up a saved search. Even if you get one, you can leave the search running and lowball a backup over a period of time.
Features like being able to project from an SD or compact flash card would seem to be totally awesome for you, as then it could operate without a computer.

If mine sounds interesting, but you'd like to see it in action just lemme know.
